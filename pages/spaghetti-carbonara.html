Spaghetti Carbonara

20 Min.
Easy
17.06.2024
The value must be between 1 and 20

Preparation

approx. 20 Min.
Total Time approx. 20 Min.
  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and add plenty of salt. Cook the spaghetti al dente according to package instructions.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, cut the pancetta or guanciale into small cubes. Heat a pan over medium heat and fry the pancetta without adding any fat until crispy. Optionally, you can add a peeled clove of garlic for extra flavor while frying. Remove the garlic clove after frying.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the egg with grated Parmesan or Pecorino. Season with some salt and plenty of freshly ground pepper.
  4. Drain the spaghetti, reserving some of the cooking water.
  5. Immediately add the drained spaghetti to the pan with the hot pancetta and mix well. Remove the pan from the heat and add the egg mixture while stirring constantly so that the egg doesn't scramble but creates a creamy sauce. If needed, add some of the reserved pasta water to make the sauce smoother.
  6. Serve the carbonara immediately, optionally topped with additional grated cheese and freshly ground pepper.
