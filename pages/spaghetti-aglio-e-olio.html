Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

20 Min.
Simple

The value must be between 1 and 20

Preparation

approx. 15 Min.
Total Time approx. 20 Min.
  1. First, cook the spaghetti al dente in plenty of salted water according to package instructions. Meanwhile, peel the garlic and slice it thinly. Finely chop the chili pepper (or prepare dried chili flakes) and set aside.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Carefully saut√© the garlic until lightly golden brown - not too dark, or it will become bitter. Then add the chili and briefly toast together.
  3. Drain the cooked spaghetti, reserving about half a cup of pasta water. Add the spaghetti directly to the pan with the garlic-chili oil. Add some pasta water and toss well until the oil is well distributed and slightly emulsified.
  4. Sprinkle with chopped parsley if desired and serve immediately - optionally with some freshly grated parmesan.
