Lasagna Bolognese

90 Min.
Medium
17.06.2024
The value must be between 1 and 20

Preparation

approx. 45 Min.
Total Time approx. 90 Min.
  1. First, prepare the Bolognese sauce: Peel and finely chop the onions and garlic. Peel and grate the carrots. Then heat oil in a pot, brown the ground meat evenly, add onions, garlic, and carrots, and continue to sauté. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, ketchup, basil, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper, and simmer on lowest heat for about 10 minutes.
  2. Next, prepare the béchamel sauce: Melt butter in a pot, add flour and immediately stir with a whisk. Now slowly add the milk while stirring constantly. Bring to a slow boil and stir until the sauce thickens. Then season with pepper, salt, and nutmeg. For the lasagna, grease a baking dish with olive oil. Now alternate layers of lasagna sheets and Bolognese sauce. Start with the lasagna sheets, then the sauce, etc., alternating layers.
  3. Finally, top with the béchamel sauce and add freshly grated Gouda cheese. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (356°F) with fan for about 30 minutes.
