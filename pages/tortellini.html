Preparation

approx. 25 Min.
Total Time approx. 25 Min.
  1. Cook the tortellini in salted water according to package instructions. Meanwhile, peel and finely chop the onion and garlic. Optional: Prepare mushrooms or spinach if you want to enhance the sauce.
  2. Heat some oil in a large pan. Saut√© the onion and garlic until translucent. Then add the cream and let it simmer on medium heat. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. If desired, stir in grated parmesan or cream cheese to make the sauce creamier.
  3. Drain the cooked tortellini and add them directly to the sauce in the pan. Mix everything well and let it sit on low heat for 1-2 minutes so the tortellini can absorb the flavors.
  4. Before serving, sprinkle with fresh parsley and some grated parmesan if desired.
